Welcome to Sherwood Observatory

Discover the wonders
of astronomy

Fantastic for all ages! Learn about the wonders of the cosmos, experience the scale of the universe in our wrap-around planetarium shows, and stargaze through our observatory telescopes.

Let us take you on a journey of discovery to find out how we watch and listen to space, what we know about the galaxies beyond, and what is yet to be discovered!

See the night sky whatever the weather with our magical planetarium shows.

Open Days

During the Spring and Summer, the longer evenings make it difficult to view objects through our telescopes. Instead, we hold a number of solar viewing events during the daytime to share our facilities and introduce astronomy to visitors.
